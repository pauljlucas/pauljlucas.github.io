Currently, I am only playing on my own but if you are interested in getting together to play please email me!

Video Games

Video games have always been a fun getaway for me and I can honestly say without my interest in video games my early interest in coding would not have been there. Growing up I was constantly impressed by mods and how single people were able to add these extra features to games just by writing code. I think reading about the mod Desert Storm for Battlefield 1942 was probably the first time I was introduced to coding and the possibilities. Now, I enjoy playing Sport games (NHL, Fifa and Madden mainly) with buddies at my apartment and will crack open my ever expanding Steam library once in a while...and probably pass over one of the 20 games I still have yet to play, in usual Steam fashion.
